History of volleyballbatman

Volleyball is a famous sport played from everywhere around the world. It's also a fun sport to play if you're at a beach with family and friends! The sport volleyball was created in 1895 and became known as an olympic sport in 1964. Volleyball, at the time, was known as a relaxed and alternative version of basketball. Beach volleyball was also introduced to the Summer Olympics in 1992 as a demonstartion event, it became an official Summer Olympic sport in 1996. The USA is the only country to win medals in almost every edition.

How to play volleyball

Volleyball is played by two opposing teams with a maximum of 6 people per team, you can also play with smaller teams of four or two if you'd like. The ball must be served under or over hand and has to be seen clearly by the opponents before the actual serve. To score a point, the ball must hit the opponents side of the ground.
